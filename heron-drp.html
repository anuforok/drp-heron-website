How To Request HERON Access

STEPS

1

Sponsorship Request

On the Heron home page, click on "HERON Sponsorship Request" under Regulatory Requests.

2

Sponsoree(s)

Enter the first and last name or username for sponsoree(s)

3

IRB Protocol Study

Look up study id, then select all approved team members and click done

4

Sponsorship and Data Access Form

Complete the HERON sponsorship and data access form and click submit.

If you already have access to HERON then sign in by clicking the START USING HERON button below
